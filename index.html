Welcome to Abyss Maker, a real-time currency exchange rate checker to provide you with fast and accurate results.

Background History

Abyss Maker was made in December 2020, by Beak Wei Xiang, a student in Immersive Media of Ngee Ann Polytechnic. He made this website for his Assignment 2 of Interactive Development course during his studies. He made use of the free API provided by fixer.io to make this website possible. Currently, this website is still running. He hopes that users of this website would find it useful and make things more convenient for them. Enjoy!
